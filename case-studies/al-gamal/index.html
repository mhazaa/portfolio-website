Al-Gamal - 2023

Branding and Website Build

Website Repository

Al-Gamal for Engineering was established in 1999 in Sana'a, Yemen. I was recently tasked to design a complete rebrand of the business as well as building a frontend website for it. I took a lot of inspiration from Yemen's architectural style and tried to marry the ornate, rich look of the Old City of Sana'a's buildings with a more minimalist, modern aesthetic.

After finishing the style guide for this project, and designing the logo, business card, promotional brochures, and the website, I began the build. Given this was largely a single-page front-end website, I mirrored the minimalist modernism of the visuals on the engineering side. I didn't use any technology that wasn't necessary, but I elected to keep a basic though modern architecture of Typescript and Sass to provide easy scalability in the future, both compiled separately without a bundler as the project is relatively small.
